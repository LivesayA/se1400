    • Website Summary:

    What content will the site contain?

    The site will mostly contain information about myself in order to be shown as a resume for potential employers in the future.

    Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

    More than likely it will be from royalty free websites or self-generated imagery that I can use in a resume to a future employer.

    Why should users care about the focus of the site?

    Because the focus of the site is more for my personal use in getting a job and reflecting myself as a person and may hold little or no interest to the person viewing it.

    Why should a site be built for this?

    While a standard resume is perfectly fine, creating your own website using html that is customized is impressive, especially if the UI is clean or well thought out.

    What would you like users to do at your site?

    I would like users to look at the site, view code and other things about me to get a good idea of my abilities and skills, and come to the decision to hire me.

    What is the goal of the site?

    The goal of the site is to both polish my skills in html and create a living website that I can consistently update as I go through my career.
