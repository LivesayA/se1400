    • Geolocation:

    The website will largely be available most likely only in the United States, as I'm not sure who else would look at it internationally, but there might be a way to translate for other languages in case I don't stay in the US.

    Age:

    The age of the viewer might not matter as much because this would be viewed by a company, so I think the age range for those looking at the site would be between 20-50 years old.

    Gender:

    Gender probably wouldn't matter as much either but I'd primarily try to make the website aesthetically pleasing to all genders in order to increase my odds of being hired/looked at for a position.

    Other:

    Most companies today tend to have understaffed and overworked departments working through the hiring process and might not even recieve any attention, thus it would be a good idea to create an advanced UI and website to ensure hiring companies that do look at it are suitably impressed.
